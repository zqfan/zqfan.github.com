- screen -R：恢复会话，如果不存在，创建新的会话
- screen -ls：列出会话
```
$ sl
There are screens on:
    20305.hello  (Attached)
1 Sockets in /var/run/screen/S-zhiqiangfan.
```
- screen -wipe 20305：删除死掉的会话进程，如果失败，可以手动kill -9 20305再删除
